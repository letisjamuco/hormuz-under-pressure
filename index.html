CH·04 · HORMUZ vs THE WORLD

Why does Hormuz matter more than any other chokepoint?

There are 28 maritime chokepoints tracked by IMF PortWatch. Hormuz is not always the busiest by total ships — but when you look at tanker traffic and energy capacity, it stands apart. This section shows you where each chokepoint sits and how they compare.
